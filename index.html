OUR VILLA

TIGA POOL VILLA

TIGA POOL VILLA is the ideal retreat for couples and families, thoughtfully designed for relaxation and connection. Inspired by the chic aesthetics of Bali, the interiors are carefully curated to offer the perfect blend of style and comfort, ensuring a luxurious and welcoming stay.

The villa features a 26-foot swimming pool, six queen beds, two super single beds, and two foldable beds, accommodating up to 16 guests. Additional amenities include a BBQ pit, one bathtub, three toilets, five indoor parking spaces, as well as a premium Netflix subscription and a karaoke system for endless entertainment.
